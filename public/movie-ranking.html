The Best Movies According to Armaan

My top 3 movies of All-Time.

Taare Zameen Par

"Taare Zameen Par" (Like Stars on Earth) is a 2007 Indian drama film that tells the story of Ishaan Awasthi, an 8-year-old dyslexic child who struggles in the traditional Indian school system.

Tiger Zinda Hai

Tiger Zinda Hai is a Hindi action thriller film, a sequel to Ek Tha Tiger, that follows a daring rescue mission in Iraq.

Baby Drive

A young, partially hearing-impaired getaway driver who relies on music to be the best in his dangerous profession.
